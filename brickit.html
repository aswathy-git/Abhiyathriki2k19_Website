Make it, brick it!

The chance to give a redemption to your creative side. A plan to give your creativity a chance of collaboration with the structural engineering. Bricks glue together to end up into a massive structure. We will give you bricks, you will give us the masterpiece of your art. Join hands with the event presented to you by the Department of Civil Engineering, NITPY. Grab your chance to make it big. BRICKIT!!

Dates: 1 March, 2019

The rules for the event are :

  • Team event. A team can only have a maximum of 3 members.
  • The event consists of 3 rounds.
  • Each round has different conditions which are given to the contestants before the initiation of each round.
  • Eliminations will be conducted round-wise for those who don't adhere to the conditions provided.
  • Not more than 3 teams of the same college are allowed to participate.
  • Participants must report at the venue atleast 15 minutes before the commencement of the event.
  • Competition head / coordinator has the right to disqualify any team in any case.
  • Judges will analyze performance. Results will be final and unobjectionable
  • Maximum two teams will be allowed from one college.
  • Each team must have atleast one Civil Engineer.

Registration Cost: Rs. 10/per team
